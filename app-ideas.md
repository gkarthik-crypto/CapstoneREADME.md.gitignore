# App Ideas

## Top 3 App Ideas

### 1. Daily Routine Tracker
- Track daily tasks such as gym, studying, and personal goals
- Simple checklist interface for marking tasks complete
- Streak counter to encourage consistency
- Notifications and reminders for unfinished tasks

---

### 2. Quote or Motivation App
- Displays a random quote each day
- Allows users to save favorite quotes
- Organizes quotes into categories (success, discipline, motivation, etc.)
- Clean and simple user interface for daily inspiration

---

### 3. Gym Workout Tracker
- Log exercises, reps, and sets
- Track workout progress over time
- View history of workouts
- Graph strength improvements and performance trends
